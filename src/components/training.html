Training for a Career in Cybersecurity

Securing, protecting, and defending our nation’s cybersecurity data and infrastructure requires a responsive, extensive, and highly trained workforce.

The NICCS Education and Training Catalog is the place to start for all people interested in advancing their cybersecurity education: current cybersecurity employees looking to build skills, career changers seeking to forge a new path or veterans hoping to retrain for the next phase in life.

The NICCS Education and Training Catalog offers more than 3,000 cybersecurity-related courses throughout the country, both online and in person from over 125 different providers. Visit the Education and Training Catalog today and find the course(s) that paves the way to a future in a cybersecurity career.

Free Government Cybersecurity Training

The Federal Virtual Training Environment (FedVTE) is a free online, on-demand cybersecurity training system for government personnel and veterans. FedVTE contains more than 800 hours of training on topics such as ethical hacking and surveillance, risk management, and malware analysis courses ranging from beginner to advanced levels. Learn more about FedVTE.
